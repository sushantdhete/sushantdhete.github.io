// Biography

Building the future, one commit at a time

Hello! I'm Sushant Ashok Dhete, an AI Developer, Web Developer, and Automation Enthusiast from Nagpur, India. I'm passionate about the intersection of artificial intelligence and practical software engineering — building tools that are not just technically impressive, but genuinely useful.

My journey in tech started with a curiosity about how computers work and evolved into a deep fascination with how machines can learn and automate. Today, I build everything from intelligent web applications and automated content pipelines to algorithmic trading infrastructure on AWS.

When I'm not coding, I'm researching market microstructure, exploring new AI models, or reading about systems design. I believe in continuous learning and building in public — sharing what I know and learning from the community.
